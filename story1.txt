Once upon a time, there was an old man and woman.
The old man went to a mountain to cut lawn and the old woman went to a river to wash her clothes.
She found a big peach floating in the river. 
She caught the big peach and brought it home.
He wanted to eat the big peach as it was looked very tasty. 
She found a baby inside when she cut the peach.
The boy was named Momotaro because he was born from a peach.
